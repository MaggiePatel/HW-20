Only one in every three people receive treatment for their anxiety disorders.

The Problem

Anxiety disorders and specific phobias can develop for a number of reasons. They can be genetic or caused by life events or chronic stress.  The type of treatment used to treat specific fears like flying requires small exposure steps. Exposure therapy as an intervention for a fear of flying is merely impossible due to travel security procedures and the cost of flying.  Millions of people suffer from fears related to flying when these fears can be treated and prevented.

The Solution

Let’s create accessibility for the treatment of a fear of flying the right way. Yoke allows users to learn about their anxiety, practice skills, and be rewarded for effort vs. outcome.  Users will also begin to normalize their symptoms by connecting with other users who experience the same problem. ​
